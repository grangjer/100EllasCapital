THIS IS 100 ELLAS

A space shaped by real experiences and practical insight, created to help you build your own impact investing roadmap—so you can engage with capital with clarity.

We start from a simple belief: capital shapes outcomes.

Why 100 Ellas?

Because one Ella is powerful — but 100 Ellas is a multiplier.

“Ellas,” rooted in the feminine “she,” represents many women stepping into capital allocation. The idea is simple: when more women move from observers to allocators, the impact compounds.

What does intentional capital allocation look like in practice?

Supporting regenerative systems that strengthen people and planet, advancing critical research, or backing solutions to systemic challenges across sectors.
